Dogs are sometimes referred to as man's best friend because they are kept as domestic pets and are usually loyal and like being around humans.
They are also helpful in reducing stress, anxiety, and depression, loneliness, encourage exercise and playfulness and even improve your cardiovascular health.
Pets or companion animals are animals raised primarily for entertainment or personal companionship, not as working animals.
Pets provide physical and emotional benefits to their owners (or "guardians") . Dog walking can provide exercise, fresh air, and social interaction for people and dogs.
